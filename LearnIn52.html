A Prototype?!

What does this mean?!

A brief introduction

Our main goal in building this prototype was to get a very simple looking design that would be very easy to work with and fast to get through so no time feels wasted. We didn't want you to be lost or stuck at any point.

How it works

There are 5 Yes or No questions that will give you feedback adequate to your answer. We wanted people who are doing something wrong to know why and how to fix it but we also wanted to give the people who are already on the right track an extra bit of information that would help them improve.

What's in it for you

For the moment there is no prize, except for self-improvement. When you finish the quiz you will be ranked based on your answers against other users on your same age range and also on the same location to give you a feel of the community and your place in it, but feel free to suggest other rewards that you would like to get and if possible, we will implement them in the future! As we keep on saying, we care about you and we want you to feel appreciated and that participating feels good for you!
